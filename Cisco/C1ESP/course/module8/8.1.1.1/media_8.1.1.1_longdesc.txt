Figure one is an interactive media element that allows the learner to enter any character and have it translated to the corresponding A.S.C.I.I. bit value.  For example a capital letter A is converted to the following binary byte pattern, zero one zero zero zero zero zero one. Figure two shows how the decimal number one hundred ninety two is formed.  It is the combination of one set of the hundreds position, nine sets of the tens position, and two ones from the ones position.
