Figure lists objectives for the chapter. These objectives include the learner being able to explain how networks affect the way we interact, learn, work and play, to be able to explain the concept of a converged network, to explain the use of network devices.  The learner will be able to explain the basic structure of the Internet, explain how LANs and WANs interconnect to the Internet and be able to explain the impact of B.Y.O.D., online collaboration, video, and cloud computing on a business network.
