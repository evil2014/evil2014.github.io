Figure lists the advantages and disadvantages of a peer to peer network.  The advantages are they are easy to set up, they have less complexity, they are lower cost because network devices and dedicated servers may not be required, and they can be used for simple tasks such as transferring files and sharing printers.  The disadvantages are they have no centralized administration, they are not as secure, they are not scalable, and all devices may act as both clients and servers which can slow their performance.
