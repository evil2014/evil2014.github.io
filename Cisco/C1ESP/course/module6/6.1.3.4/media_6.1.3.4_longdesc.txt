Figure one is an interactive activity that allows the learner to practice matching a description of an I.P. header field with its name.  The fields in this activity are version, time to live, source I.P. address, destination I.P. address, differentiated services and protocol. Figure two is an interactive activity that allows the learner to practice matching a description of more I.P. header fields with their name.  The fields in this activity are Internet header length, total length and header checksum.
