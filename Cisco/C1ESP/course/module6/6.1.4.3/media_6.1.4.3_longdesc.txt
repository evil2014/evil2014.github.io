Figure shows an I.P. version 4 header.  There are fields shaded in yellow (version, source I.P. address and destination I.P. address) that were kept in I.P. version 6.  There are fields shaded in green (type of service, total length, time to live and protocol) that were renamed or repositioned in I.P. version 6 and the fields shaded in gray were dropped in I.P. version 6.
