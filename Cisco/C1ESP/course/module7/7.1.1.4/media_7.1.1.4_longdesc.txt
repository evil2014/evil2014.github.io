The figure shows the four layers of the T.C.P.I.P. model and lists protocols that work at each of the layers.  At the Application layer there is F.T.P., H.T.T.P., S.M.T.P., D.N.S., and T.F.T.P.  At the Transport layer there is both T.C.P. and U.D.P.  The Internet layer has I.P. and the Network Access layer has LAN and WAN connections.
