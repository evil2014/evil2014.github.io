Figure shows the command used to set the boot environment variable to the i.o.s. on the switch starting in global configuration mode. The command is, boot system flash colon slash c 2 9 6 0 hyphen lanbase k 9 hyphen m z dot 1 5 0 hyphen 2 dot S E slash c 2 9 6 0 hyphen lanbase k 9 hyphen m z dot 1 5 0 hyphen 2 dot S E dot bin.  The storage location is flash, the name after the first slash is a folder in flash memory, and the name after the second slash is the i.o.s. filename.
