Figure one shows a topology of one switch with a router, a computer, and a d.h.c.p.v.6. server connected.  Figure one shows the computer sending a router solicitation message and the router sending a router advertisement in response. Figure two shows the client computer sending out a d.h.c.p.v.6. solicitation message looking for a d.h.c.p.v.6. server on the local link. Figure three shows the d.h.c.p.v.6. server sending out an advertisement unicast message in response to the solicitation message. Figure five shows the d.h.c.p.v.6. server sending the appropriate information to the client in a reply unicast message.
